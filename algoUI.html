Algorithmic Thinking

Suppose you have a bag of red, yellow, and blue chips; the number of chips of each kind in the bag is unknown. Consider a process where you repeatedly perform the following steps. If only one chip remains in the bag, you take it out. Otherwise, you remove two chips at random.

• If one of the two removed chips is red, you do not put any chips in the bag

• If both removed chips are yellow, you put yellow chip(s) and blue chip(s) in the bag

• If one of the chips is blue and the other is not red, you put red chip(s) in the bag
